The Event

NodeConf London is a one-day, single-track conference.

NodeConf London will bring together our industry's enterprise heavyweights, our subject experts and highlight the finest talent London has to offer.

We’ll bring you perspectives from the enterprise whilst providing cutting edge developer insights.

Presented by

Cause

All profits from NodeConf London 2016 will be used to support the Node.js community here in London. If you would like to waive your 'Speaker Fee', we will add it to the overall donation, and if you have an alternative charity or non-profit organization that is close to your heart, we can arrange the donation on your behalf.

We will proudly be supporting NodeGirls, NodeBots and NodeSchool London.

The Barbican Centre is located within walking distance from Barbican and Moorgate.

Rail

The nearest train stations are Liverpool Street and Farringdon.

Bus

Bus Route 153 runs directly past the Barbican along Chiswell Street.

Bicycle

Our nearest TFL bike station is Silk Street.

Cycle racks are available at the entrance to Car Park 5 and exit to Car Park 3 and helmets and equipment can be left in our free cloakrooms.
